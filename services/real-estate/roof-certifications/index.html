How It Works

  1. Site evaluation and photo documentation
  2. List of corrective items (if needed)
  3. Corrections completed (by us or others)
  4. Verification visit and certification issuance

Typical Qualification Criteria

  • ✓ Roof is watertight with reasonable remaining life
  • ✓ No active leaks or critical defects
  • ✓ Flashings/drains/penetrations serviceable

FAQ

What if repairs are needed?

We can quote and perform corrections, or you may use others. Certification is issued once items are verified completed.

How long is certification valid?

Depends on lender/insurer requirements. We can customize language if requested.
