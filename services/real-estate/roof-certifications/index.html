How It Works

  1. On-site evaluation and photo documentation
  2. List of corrective items, if any are required
  3. Corrections completed (by us or by others)
  4. Verification and certification issuance once criteria are met

Certification is issued only if the roof meets qualification criteria. Corrective work, if needed, is separate.

Typical Qualification Criteria

  • ✓ No active leaks or critical defects
  • ✓ Flashings, penetrations, and drains are serviceable
  • ✓ Reasonable remaining service life

FAQ

What if repairs are needed?

We’ll note the items. You can hire us or others to complete them. Once finished, we verify and issue the certification if the roof now meets criteria.

Is this the same as a full report?

No. This is a certification for underwriting. If your lender or insurer requests detailed photos and commentary, please book a Roof Report instead.
