FAQ

How do I choose between Certification and Report?

If the insurer only needs a pass/fail letter, choose Certification ($395). If they want photos and detailed notes, choose the Report ($495).

What if issues prevent certification?

We’ll note items required to certify. If you authorize those repairs, we can re-inspect and issue certification once the roof meets requirements.

Is a site visit required?

Yes—these documents are based on our inspection. We’ll coordinate access and timing with you or your agent.
