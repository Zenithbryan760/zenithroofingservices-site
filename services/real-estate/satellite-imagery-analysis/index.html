What We Check

From Imagery

  • Approximate roof area & geometry
  • Pitch indicators & complexity cues
  • Obstructions: trees, solar, skylights, dormers
  • Access, edges, and safety considerations

What We Infer (with Caution)

  • Likely material type & layering
  • Condition trends (staining, patchwork)
  • Areas needing closer look on-site

Imagery helps scope and budgeting. It’s not a substitute for on-site findings, certification, or a full report.

Limitations

Satellite/aerial images can be dated or obstructed (trees, overhangs, solar arrays). Multi-structure properties or complex roofs may require an on-site visit. If you need documentation for lenders/insurers, book a Roof Report or Roof Certification.
