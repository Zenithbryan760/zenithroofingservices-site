How It Works

  1. You submit the address and a few details and pay the $95 online estimate fee.
  2. We measure and review the roof using satellite imagery and available data.
  3. We send a line-item estimate (repair or replacement) you can share in your transaction.
  4. If you proceed with Zenith, the $95 is applied as a credit on the job.

If imagery is obstructed or insufficient, we may request extra photos or recommend an on-site service.

What We Need

  • Property address (and unit, if applicable)
  • Your role: buyer, seller, or agent
  • Goal: repair budget, replacement, or compare options
  • Any known issues (leaks, age, prior work)
  • Optional: recent photos or inspection excerpts

Deliverable

  • Line-item estimate with scope notes
  • Assumptions based on imagery & public data
  • Options (e.g., materials/upgrades) when useful

This is an estimate, not a certification or full report. For documentation, see our Roof Report or Roof Certification.

Limitations

Remote estimates rely on imagery that may be outdated or partially obstructed (trees, solar arrays, covered patios). Multi-structure properties and complex roofs may require an on-site visit (Visual Inspection or Roof Report).
