5" vs 6" K-Style — Which Size Do I Need?

Home ConditionRecommended Size
Typical roof areas & pitches5" K-style (most homes)
Large roof planes or steep pitch6" K-style (higher capacity)
Long runs with valley dumps6" near valleys/downspouts
Frequent heavy rain exposure6" for extra margin

We’ll verify sizing on site. Mixed sizing (5" + targeted 6") is common for optimal performance and look.

FAQ

Do I need 5" or 6" gutters?

Most homes use 5". For big roof planes, steep pitch, or long runs with valley flow, we’ll recommend 6" for capacity.

How are gutters attached?

We use hidden hangers every 24"–32" and fasten into structure for strength and a clean look.

Can you match my home’s color?

Yes—many factory finishes are available. We’ll bring a color sheet so you can pick on site.
