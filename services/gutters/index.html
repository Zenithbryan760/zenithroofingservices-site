Gutters & Guards — FAQ

What’s the difference between 5" and 6" gutters?
5" gutters fit most homes with standard rooflines and rainfall. 6" gutters carry more water, ideal for big roof areas or steep pitches.
Do aluminum gutters hold up near the coast?
Yes. Aluminum resists rust and is lightweight. With correct fasteners and sealants, it performs well in coastal Southern California.
Will guards stop all cleaning?
They drastically reduce it, but an occasional rinse/inspection keeps everything working perfectly.
