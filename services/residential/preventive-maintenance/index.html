Our Cleaning Methods

We match method to roof material and moss severity—always gentle, always roof-safe.

Soft-Wash • Low-Pressure

Biodegradable cleaner applied at low pressure, then gently rinsed. Ideal for most asphalt shingles and many tile roofs with algae/black streaks.

Manual Moss Removal

Careful hand removal of moss/lichen where needed, using roof-appropriate tools that don’t gouge or abrade the surface.

Preventative Treatment

Optional post-clean treatment and recommendations (including zinc/copper strategies) to slow future growth in shaded or coastal zones.

FAQ

How long do results last?

Sun/shade, trees, and marine air all matter. Most roofs look great for 1–3 years; shaded/coastal areas may benefit from periodic touch-ups or preventative treatment.

Do you clean clay and concrete tile?

Yes. We tune the process for tile so there’s no glaze damage or water intrusion.

Will you find problems during cleaning?

If we see slipped tiles, missing caps, or sealant failures, we’ll document and recommend repair options.
