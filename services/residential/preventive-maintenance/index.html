Smart DIY Between Visits

  • ✓ Keep trees trimmed back from the roof
  • ✓ Clear ground-level downspout exits
  • ✓ After big winds, walk the perimeter and look for slipped tiles or shingle tabs

Skip roof walking if you’re not comfortable—surface damage is common. Send us photos from the ground and we’ll advise.

FAQ

Can preventive maintenance stop leaks?

It reduces risk by addressing common failure points. If we find active leaks or larger issues, we’ll quote targeted repairs.

Do you document what you did?

Yes—brief photo summary with notes and clear next steps if needed.

Will you replace broken tiles?

We reset minor items during tune-ups; larger breakage is quoted as a repair so you can approve costs first.
