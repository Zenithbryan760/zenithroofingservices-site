FAQs

How long does it take?

Depends on roof size/complexity. We provide a schedule and daily updates.

Can you match my tiles?

We source close matches when possible and place any non-exact matches in low-visibility areas.

What warranties apply?

Manufacturer documentation governs materials; our labor warranty is detailed in your proposal.

Will this stop leaks?

Lift & lay addresses the aged underlayment and details where leaks originate. We’ll note any structural issues if discovered.
