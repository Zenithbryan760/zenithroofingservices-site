Is My Roof a Candidate?

Silicone is a **restoration** system. Your roof should be structurally sound with manageable wet areas and compatible surfaces.

Good CandidatesBetter Replaced
Low-slope roofs with limited wet insulation Widespread saturated insulation or rotten deck
Existing single-ply, torch-down/modified, or coated roofs (after adhesion test) Delaminating substrates that fail adhesion tests
Minor ponding that can be managed with detailing Severe structural ponding or collapsed slopes

If your roof isn’t a fit, we’ll recommend TPO or flat roof replacement.

FAQ

Will silicone stop all leaks?

We address existing leak sources before coating and reinforce details. If the roof is too degraded, we’ll recommend replacement for a guaranteed outcome.

How long does it last?

System life and warranty depend on thickness, substrate, and maintenance. We specify mils and manufacturer programs to match your goals.

Is it slippery?

Silicone can be slick when wet. We include walkway pads or texture where appropriate.
