Our Re-Roof & Repair Process

1) Evaluate

Inspect decking, flashings, ventilation, and trouble areas.

2) Protect & Tear-Off

Landscape protection, clean tear-off, deck prep.

3) Re-Sheet & Dry-In

Replace bad decking, underlayment, and flashing upgrades.

4) Install & Vent

Architectural shingles + balanced intake/exhaust ventilation.

5) Detail & Clean

Sealants, penetrations, magnets for nails, thorough cleanup.

6) Photos & Walkthrough

Final check with photo documentation for your records.

FAQ

Can you repair instead of replace?

Yes—if the roof still has life left, we’ll target leak sources (flashings, penetrations, valleys) and provide photos and recommendations.

Do you offer financing or phased work?

We can stage work and discuss cost-smart scopes. Ask us about options for timing and phases.

Do you handle HOA requirements?

Yes—color sheets, scope summaries, and install specs are available to help with approvals.
