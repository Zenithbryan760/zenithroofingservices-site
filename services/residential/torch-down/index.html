When Torch-Down Is the Right Choice

Choose torch-down for roofs too low for shingles, for clean integration at parapet walls, or when you need robust flashing around skylights, curbs, and HVAC pads.

  • ✓ Low-slope or flat areas where shingles won’t shed water
  • ✓ Parapet walls that need continuous flashing
  • ✓ Complex penetrations: skylights, vents, pipes, equipment
  • ✓ Drainage with scuppers or internal drains

FAQ

Can you install over my existing flat roof?

Sometimes. We’ll evaluate deck condition, existing layers, and drainage before recommending overlay vs. tear-off.

Will you fix ponding areas?

We address drainage details and can recommend improvements. Mild birdbaths are common on flats and can often be managed.

Do you coat torch-down roofs?

Yes—reflective coatings can reduce heat and extend service life when applied at the right time.
