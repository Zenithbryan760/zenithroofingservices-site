Our TPO Installation Process

1) Assess & Plan

Verify deck, edges, penetrations, drainage, and insulation goals.

2) Prep Deck

Tear-off or recover (as allowed), repair sheathing, prime as needed.

3) Insulation/Taper

Install polyiso and tapered crickets to move water efficiently.

4) Membrane Install

Mechanically attached or fully adhered, then heat-weld seams.

5) Flashings & Edge

Detail pipes, skylights, walls; new edge metal and scuppers.

6) Final QA

Walkthrough, photos, and care tips for long-term performance.

FAQ

Is TPO safe to install?

Yes—seams are **hot-air welded**, so there’s **no torch flame** on your home.

Will it keep my home cooler?

White cool-roof TPO reflects sunlight to reduce heat gain. We’ll review options that meet **Title 24** when specified.

Do you work around skylights and walls?

We rebuild those details with proper curb/wall flashings and counterflashing for a clean, watertight finish.
