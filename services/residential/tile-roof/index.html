Lift & Relay — New Underlayment, Same Tiles

If your tiles still look great but the roof leaks, you probably need a **lift & relay**. We carefully remove your tiles, install modern high-performance underlayment and flashings, then reinstall the tiles (replacing damaged ones).

FAQ

Clay vs. concrete — which should I choose?

Clay offers timeless looks and longevity; concrete is budget-friendly and versatile. Underlayment quality and flashings matter most for watertightness.

Can you work around solar panels?

Yes. We coordinate safe removal/reinstall with your solar provider, protect tiles, and seal every penetration.

Will repairs blend in?

We source close-match tiles and blend placements. If discontinued, we’ll propose smart alternatives to keep curb appeal high.
