Storm Damage (Wind & Hail)

We inspect, document, and restore your roof with manufacturer‑aligned methods. We can meet your adjuster and provide supplements when appropriate.

What We Check

  • Lifted/creased or missing shingles; cracked or displaced tiles
  • Hail bruising, impact marks, accelerated wear
  • Flashing, vents, skylight seals, edge metals
  • Interior signs: stains and moisture readings as applicable

Next Steps

  1. Temporary dry‑in/tarp if needed
  2. Photo set & line‑item scope
  3. Adjuster walkthrough & supplements
  4. Code‑compliant repair or full replacement
