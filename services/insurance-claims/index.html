Insurance & Storm Damage Claims

Wind, hail, and storm events can leave hidden roof damage. We handle documentation, meet with your adjuster, and perform repairs or full replacement to code.

How We Help

  • On‑site assessment with photos & measurement
  • Written, line‑item scope aligned to code & manufacturer specs
  • Adjuster walkthrough & supplemental support
  • Temporary dry‑in/tarping if needed
  • Repair or replacement options with clear timelines

Common Storm Issues

  • Wind‑lifted shingles/tiles, broken or displaced ridges
  • Hail bruising, cracked tiles, accelerated granule loss
  • Flashing displacement, vent and skylight seal failures
  • Debris impact, falling limbs, punctures

Service Area

San Diego County, North County (Escondido, Oceanside, Carlsbad, La Jolla, etc.) and Temecula.
