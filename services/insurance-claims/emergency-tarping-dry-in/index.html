Emergency Tarping & Dry‑In

We secure the roof quickly to limit interior damage, then provide a clear plan for permanent repair or replacement.

What to Expect

  • On‑site evaluation and photos
  • Tarping or temporary membrane/underlayment where appropriate
  • Safe tie‑in around penetrations, skylights, and edges
  • Written plan for permanent remediation

Helpful Tips

  • Move valuables away from leak areas; place buckets as needed
  • Photograph interior damage for your records
  • Save invoices and reports for your claim file
