FAQ

Can you work while the property is occupied?

Yes—most of our commercial shingle projects are fully occupied. We coordinate notices, maintain access, and keep sites clean.

Do you offer cool-roof shingles?

Yes. Where Title 24 cool-roof requirements apply, we’ll propose compliant options or value-engineer alternatives if allowed.

How do you handle rain or weather delays?

We schedule smart, monitor forecasts, and never leave open exposures. Temporary protection is in place if weather shifts.
