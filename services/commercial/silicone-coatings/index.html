Is Your Roof a Candidate?

  • ✓ Sound substrate with manageable repairs
  • ✓ Limited wet insulation (core sampling as needed)
  • ✓ Slopes/drains acceptable or improved
  • ✓ Proper adhesion achieved via primer/testing
  • ✓ Details/penetrations can be reinforced
  • ✓ Building usage suits coating timelines

Why Silicone

Reflective & Cool

High SRI helps meet Title 24 and reduce heat gain.

Ponding Resistance

Silicone tolerates incidental ponding better than many coatings.

Lower Disruption

Minimal tear-off when the roof qualifies saves time and noise.

Extend Life

A new protective surface and reinforced details.

Our Process
