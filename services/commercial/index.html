FAQ

Common questions

Recover vs. tear-off—what’s right for us?

If the deck is sound and there’s a single layer, a recover can be faster and cost-effective. Trapped moisture, multiple layers, or widespread failure point to tear-off. We’ll document findings with photos and, if needed, core data.

How do you minimize disruption?

We phase sections, protect entries, set clear walk paths, coordinate deliveries, and schedule high-impact work off-hours to keep operations open.

What warranties are available?

Workmanship plus manufacturer material & labor coverage (often 15–20 years) on eligible systems installed to spec. We’ll review requirements upfront.
