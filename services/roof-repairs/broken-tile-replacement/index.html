Tile Matching & Sourcing

We identify your tile profile (manufacturer, mold, size) and color family. If exact stock is discontinued, we blend color or place substitutes strategically for a uniform look from the ground.

  • Profile ID & closest-available match
  • Color blending / painting when appropriate
  • Discrete placement of non-identical pieces

Underlayment Checks

If damage suggests paper failure, we perform localized felt/underlayment patches during the tile swap to restore waterproofing.

Our Repair Process

1) Inspect & Identify

Document cracked/slipped tiles, check flashings and felt exposure.

2) Source & Stage

Match profile/color and stage safe access paths on tile.

3) Replace & Patch

Swap damaged tiles, address localized underlayment as needed.

4) Blend & Clean

Color blend if required, tidy debris, and provide photos.

FAQ

Can you walk on my tile safely?

We use tile-safe paths and pads and train techs on profile-specific footing to prevent breakage.

How many tiles should I replace?

We replace all visibly cracked/slipped pieces in the affected areas and recommend preventive swaps if others are at risk.

Will the new tiles stand out?

We match as close as possible; when exact color is unavailable, we blend or place tiles strategically to maintain curb appeal.
