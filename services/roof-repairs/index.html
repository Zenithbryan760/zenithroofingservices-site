Common Roof Repair Symptoms

Ceiling Stains

Brown rings, bubbling paint, or damp drywall—often from flashings, skylights, or valleys.

Active Drips

During storms or after irrigation—pinpoint and fix the source before damage spreads.

Loose / Missing

Shingles or tiles displaced by wind, age, or foot traffic.

Ponding / Blisters

Flat roof ponding, soft spots, or membrane blisters.

Skylight Leaks

Wet drywall around skylights or curb flashing failures.

Valley Overflows

Debris in valleys or roof-to-gutter transitions causing back-ups.
