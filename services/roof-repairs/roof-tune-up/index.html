What We Fix on Tile Roofs

  • Cracked or slipped tiles replaced and secured
  • Leak tracing with strategic lifts and testing
  • Flashing repairs at walls, chimneys, skylights, and pipes
  • Valley debris cleanouts and flow restoration
  • Ridge & mortar fixes where present
  • Eligible underlayment patches for localized issues

We start by diagnosing the source—then repair tiles, flashings, and drainage to keep water moving off the roof. If the underlayment is failing across large areas, we’ll recommend a Tile Lift & Lay to reset the system without buying new tiles.

Repair or Tile Lift & Lay?

Good Candidates for Repair

  • Isolated cracked/slipped tiles
  • Localized flashing issue (wall, pipe, skylight)
  • Debris-related valley overflow
  • Underlayment generally sound

Consider Tile Lift & Lay

  • Widespread underlayment aging or brittleness
  • Recurring leaks across multiple planes
  • Older felt with failing flashings
  • Want a long-term reset using your existing tiles

Our Repair Process

  1. Schedule & access: safety and tile protection come first.
  2. Diagnose: trace the source, lift tiles where needed, confirm scope.
  3. Repair: replace tiles, reseal/rebuild flashings, clear valleys, patch underlayment (if eligible).
  4. Photo summary: before/after photos with brief notes for your records.

Tile Repair FAQs

Can you match my tile?

Often yes for concrete; clay availability varies by profile/color/age. If exact match isn’t available, we’ll discuss best-fit or donor-tile options.

Do you repair underlayment?

Localized patches are possible. If the felt is broadly brittle, we’ll recommend Tile Lift & Lay for a durable fix.
