Repair or Tile Lift & Lay?

Good Candidates for Repair

  • ✓ Isolated cracked/slipped tiles
  • ✓ Localized flashing issue (wall, pipe, skylight)
  • ✓ Debris-related valley overflow
  • ✓ Underlayment generally sound

Consider Tile Lift & Lay

  • ✓ Widespread underlayment aging or brittleness
  • ✓ Recurring leaks across multiple planes
  • ✓ Older felt with failing flashings
  • ✓ You want a long-term reset without new tiles

FAQs

Can you match my tile?

Often yes for concrete; clay availability varies by profile/color/age. If exact match is unavailable, we discuss best-fit or donor-tile options.

Will you walk on my tiles?

Where access requires stepping on tiles, we use tile-safe paths and pads. Some fragile clay requires additional precautions or lift solutions.

Do you repair underlayment?

Localized patches are possible. If the felt is broadly brittle, we’ll recommend Tile Lift & Lay for a durable fix.

What about birds and debris?

We can add bird-stops or screens in problem areas, and clear valleys/gutter entries to restore flow.
