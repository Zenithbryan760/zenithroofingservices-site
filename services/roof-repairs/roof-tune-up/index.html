FAQ

How often should I schedule one?

Annually is typical; twice a year for homes under trees, near the coast, or with complex rooflines.

Will you walk on my tiles/shingles?

Where access requires stepping on the roof, we use roof-safe paths and pads. Fragile materials may require additional precautions.

Do you cover my city?

Yes—North County San Diego, Temecula/Murrieta, the rest of San Diego County, and nearby Southern California communities.
