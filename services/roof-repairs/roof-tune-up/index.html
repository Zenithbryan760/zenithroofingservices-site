What’s Included in a Roof Tune-Up

  • ✓

    Seal roof penetrations (plumbing pipes, vents, satellite holes if present)

  • ✓

    Tighten or reseat flashing details at walls, skylights, and chimneys

  • ✓

    Clear debris from valleys and gutter entry points

  • ✓

    Secure loose materials (shingles/tiles/metal edges) where safely accessible

  • ✓

    Replace a limited number of small broken pieces (when available on site)

  • ✓

    Verify drain pathways for proper flow off the roof

  • ✓

    Provide a concise photo summary with notes

  • ✓

    Recommendations if broader repair or replacement is warranted

Heads up: If we find widespread underlayment failure (common on older tile roofs), we’ll recommend a Tile Lift & Lay or a targeted repair plan instead of a simple Tune-Up.
