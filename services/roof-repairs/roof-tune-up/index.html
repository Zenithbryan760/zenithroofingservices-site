Roof Tune-Up FAQs

What exactly do you do during a tune-up?

We focus on the high-value maintenance items: reseal critical flashings, tighten small loose details, clear valleys, and refresh targeted sealants—plus a short photo summary.

How often should I schedule one?

Annually is common. Twice a year is smart for homes under trees, near the coast, or with complex rooflines.

Do you cover my city?

Yes—North County San Diego, Temecula/Murrieta, the rest of San Diego County, and nearby Southern California communities.
