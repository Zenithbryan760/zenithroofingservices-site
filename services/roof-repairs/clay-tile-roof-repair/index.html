FAQs

Can you match my tile exactly?

We try to source close color/shape matches. If an exact match isn’t available, we place replacements in less-visible areas when possible.

Will repairs stop all future leaks?

Repairs address the documented issue. If underlayment is at end-of-life broadly, we may recommend a larger lift-&-lay scope.
