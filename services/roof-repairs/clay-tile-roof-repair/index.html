Common Leak Sources on Tile Roofs

Valleys & Debris

Debris dams water under tile; we clear valleys and adjust diverters.

Penetrations

Aged or loose flashing at pipes, skylights, and attic fans is a frequent culprit.

Roof-to-Wall

Kickout flashing missing? We add or correct it to protect stucco and siding.

Underlayment

Old felt or torn underlayment can require localized replacement.
