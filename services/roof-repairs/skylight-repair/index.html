Common Skylight Leak Symptoms

  • ✓ Drips at corners or after steady rain
  • ✓ Staining/bubbling paint on ceiling or shaft
  • ✓ Drafts or visible gaps at flashing
  • ✓ Cracked/crazed acrylic dome or fogged glass
  • ✓ Ponding at uphill side of curb
  • ✓ Water trails at drywall or trim

How long does a replacement take?
