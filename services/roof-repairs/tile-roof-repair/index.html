Common Tile Roof Leak Symptoms

  • ✓ Ceiling stains after wind-driven rain
  • ✓ Drips near chimneys, skylights, or walls
  • ✓ Overflow at valleys during heavy storms
  • ✓ Slipped/broken tiles or exposed underlayment
  • ✓ Cracked mortar at ridges/hips
  • ✓ Debris piles in valleys blocking flow
  • ✓ Aging/dry underlayment (brittle or torn)
  • ✓ Wind damage (lifted courses, missing pieces)

What We Repair on Tile Roofs

Flashings & Valleys

We rebuild problem transitions, clear blockages, and reseal penetrations (pipes, vents, skylights).

  • Valley cleanout & re-detail
  • Headwall/sidewall re-flash
  • Pipe, skylight, chimney flashing repairs

Tiles & Surface Issues

Replace broken/slipped tiles and address cracked mortar or displaced ridge/hip pieces.

  • Tile replacement & blending
  • Reset slipped courses
  • Ridge/hip mortar touch-ups or re-bed

Underlayment Failures

Localized underlayment repairs when feasible. If aged out widely, see Lift & Relay.

  • Localized membrane patches
  • Nail hole & penetration sealing
  • Transition reinforcement

Skylight & Wall Leaks

We correct curb details, pan flashings, and sidewall steps that cause chronic leaks.

  • Skylight curb re-flash
  • Wall step flashings
  • Counterflashing adjustments
If your roof has multiple leak points or brittle felt across large areas, a Tile Lift & Lay (Reset) may be the smart fix.

How We Repair Tile Roofs

1) Diagnose

Pinpoint the source (not just the stain). We test suspect transitions and confirm flow paths.

2) Protect & Access

Tile-safe access, padded paths, and staging to avoid collateral damage.

3) Repair & Detail

Fix flashings/valleys, replace tiles, address underlayment, and clear debris.

4) Verify & Document

Tile Roof Repair FAQs

Can you match my discontinued tiles?

We’ll source the closest match and blend patches in less visible areas for a uniform look.

Will you walk on my tiles?

We use tile-safe pads and access paths to protect fragile profiles while we work.

Is repair or lift & relay better?

Localized problems → repair. Widespread underlayment failure → lift & relay for long-term performance.
