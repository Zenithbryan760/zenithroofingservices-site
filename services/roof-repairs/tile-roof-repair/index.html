Repair or Tile Lift & Lay?

Good Candidates for Repair

  • ✓ Isolated cracked or slipped tiles
  • ✓ Localized flashing issue (wall/chimney/skylight)
  • ✓ Debris-related valley overflow
  • ✓ Underlayment generally sound

Consider Tile Lift & Lay

  • ✓ Widespread underlayment aging/brittleness
  • ✓ Recurring leaks across multiple planes
  • ✓ Older felt with failing flashings
  • ✓ You want a long-term reset without new tiles

FAQs

Can you match my tile?

Often yes for concrete. Clay profiles/colors may be limited; we’ll discuss best-fit or donor options if needed.

Will you walk on my tiles?

If roof access requires it, we use tile-safe paths/pads. Fragile clay may need additional protection or lift solutions.

Do you repair underlayment?

Localized patches are possible. Broadly brittle felt calls for Tile Lift & Lay.

Pricing?

Scope-based: tile type (concrete vs clay), height/access, broken count, flashing complexity, underlayment condition. Call or send the form for a fast quote.
