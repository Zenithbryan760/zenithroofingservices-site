FAQ

Can you match my tile?

Often yes for concrete; clay availability varies by profile/color/age. If exact match isn’t available, we discuss best-fit or donor-tile options.

Will you walk on my tiles?

We use tile-safe paths/pads where access requires stepping on tiles; fragile clay may need additional precautions.

Do you repair underlayment?

Localized patches are possible. If felt is broadly brittle, we’ll recommend Tile Lift & Lay.
