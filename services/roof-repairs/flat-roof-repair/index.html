Signs Your Flat Roof Needs Repair

  • ✓ Leaks during steady rain or after standing water
  • ✓ Open or wrinkled seams; lifted laps
  • ✓ Blisters, fishmouths, or membrane splits
  • ✓ Wet insulation, soft spots, or sagging deck
  • ✓ Clogged drains/scuppers or constant ponding
  • ✓ Cracked flashings at curbs, walls, or skylights

What We Repair on Flat Roofs

Seams & Membrane

  • Heat-weld or adhere open laps; reinforce with patches
  • Targeted membrane replacement at splits/blisters
  • Seal terminations and penetration collars

Flashings & Details

  • Rebuild curb, wall, and skylight flashings
  • Replace cracked pipe boots and pitch pans
  • Edge metal tune-ups and counterflashing integration

Systems We Service

TPO & PVC

Heat-welded single-ply systems—excellent for efficient repairs at seams and details.

Modified Bitumen / Torch-Down

SBS/APP membranes—torch, cold-process, or self-adhered; strong for targeted patches.

Built-Up & Coatings

BUR and compatible coatings for maintenance and service-life extension.

Drains & Scuppers

Reset bowls, tighten clamps, replace strainers, seal tie-ins, and improve flow.

Drainage & Ponding Solutions

Clog Removal & Tune-Up

Clear debris at drains/scuppers; verify clamp rings, strainers, and tie-ins.

Tapered Patches/Crickets

Build slope to move water off low spots and away from seams/curbs.

Detail Rework

Correct backwater laps and add diverters/saddles where helpful.

Moisture Check

Identify wet insulation and recommend targeted replacement as needed.
