Common Leak Sources on Shingle Roofs

Valleys & Transitions

Debris or misaligned shingles causes overflow—clean and reset, add diverters if needed.

Penetrations

Aging pipe boots and vent flashings crack—swap boots and reseal collars.

Walls & Chimneys

Step/counter flashing failure is common—remove, reflash, and integrate underlayment correctly.

Wind Damage

Tabs lift/tear—replace shingles and ensure proper sealing for future storms.
