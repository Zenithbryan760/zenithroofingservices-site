Why Homeowners Replace Pitched Roofs

  • ✓ Widespread shingle wear or granule loss
  • ✓ Failing tile underlayment—leaks at valleys or penetrations
  • ✓ Aging flashings and ventilation issues
  • ✓ Storm or wind damage beyond spot repairs

The Details That Matter

Ventilation

Balanced intake & ridge exhaust to protect shingles and the attic.

Flashings

New metal at penetrations, step & headwalls, and chimneys where applicable.

Valleys & Edges

Underlayment laps, ice/water at critical zones, and drip-edge for clean lines.

Deck Readiness

Replace compromised decking as needed for a solid fastening base.

Our Reroof Process

1) Inspect & Design

Confirm scope, components, ventilation, and color selections.

2) Tear-Off & Prep

Remove old layers, repair decking, and stage materials safely.

3) Install System

Underlayment, flashings, then shingles or tile relay to spec.

4) Final Detail

Sealants, ridge finish, clean-up, and photo documentation.

FAQ

Can you reuse my existing tiles?

Often yes. Usable tiles are relaid over new underlayment and flashings.

Do you help with color selections?

We’ll bring color options for shingles and coordinate with existing trim/tile where possible.

Do you handle permits?

Yes—where required, we pull permits and schedule inspections.
