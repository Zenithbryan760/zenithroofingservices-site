Flat Roof Systems We Install

TPO Single-Ply

Energy-efficient white membrane, heat-welded seams, great for SoCal UV. Good balance of value and performance.

PVC Single-Ply

Excellent chemical/grease resistance, heat-welded seams, and durability—ideal for certain commercial and residential applications.

Modified Bitumen

Torch-down or self-adhered plies for robust waterproofing and detail work around edges/penetrations.

Silicone Restoration

When the substrate is sound, a silicone coating system can restore waterproofing and extend life at lower cost.

Taper & Insulation

Improve drainage with tapered ISO boards; add R-value and help eliminate ponding around drains and low spots.

Edge Metal & Scuppers

New perimeter metals, scuppers, and overflow protection for a clean, code-compliant finish.

The Details That Keep Flat Roofs Dry

Drainage

Tapered insulation, crickets to drains, scupper placement—and we address chronic ponding areas.

Seams & Flashings

Heat-welded seams (TPO/PVC) or proper lap adhesion (mod bit). New flashings at all penetrations.

Perimeter Metals

Drip edge, gravel stops, and termination bars that match your chosen system.

Substrate Prep

Repair soft spots, fastener back-out, and blisters before the new system goes on.

FAQ

Is silicone a full replacement?

It’s a restoration coating—great when the existing roof is sound. If the membrane or deck is failing, full replacement is smarter.

White or gray membrane?

White reflects heat (energy benefits). We’ll recommend based on exposure and any HOA requirements.

Do you handle roof drains?

Yes—drain bowls, strainers, and tie-ins to the new system, plus overflow solutions as needed.
