How we work

Our replacement process

Commercial (Warehouses, Restaurants, Retail, HOA)

  1. Assessment: site visit, moisture checks, photos; cores as needed.
  2. Scope & Options: repair vs. recover vs. tear-off with drawings & cut sheets.
  3. Phasing & Safety: tenant/employee access, protection, fall-protection, hot-work controls.
  4. Permits & Inspections: Title 24 assemblies, scheduling & coordination.
  5. Execution: daily cleanup, protected paths, punch-list closeout.
  6. Warranty Packet: photos, maintenance notes, manufacturer coverage when eligible.

Residential (Single-Family)

  1. Consultation: condition review, options & materials.
  2. Proposal: line-item scope, underlayment, flashings, ventilation, and code items.
  3. Scheduling: weather window and city permit timing.
  4. Install: tear-off, deck/wood repairs if needed, new system to spec.
  5. Final QA: walkthrough, photos, warranty/maintenance guidance.
FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Recover vs. tear-off — which is better?

When the deck is sound and only one layer exists, a recover can be cost-effective and faster. Trapped moisture, multiple layers, or widespread failure point to a tear-off. We’ll document findings with photos and, if needed, core data.

What warranties are available?

We provide workmanship warranties and—on eligible systems installed to manufacturer specifications—material & labor coverage (e.g., 15–20 years). We’ll review eligibility and maintenance requirements upfront.

How do you minimize disruption to tenants or customers?

We phase work, set protected walk paths, schedule high-impact work off-hours, and keep entrances and docks usable whenever possible. Clear communication and daily housekeeping are standard.

Do you handle Title 24 and city inspections?

Yes. We design cool-roof compliant assemblies and manage permits/inspections with the city.
