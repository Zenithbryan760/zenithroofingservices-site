Our Roof Replacement Process

1) Inspect & Design

Confirm scope, ventilation, flashing details, and system choice.

2) Tear-Off & Prep

Remove old materials, repair decking, install underlayments/ice-water as needed.

3) Install System

Shingles, tile with new underlayment, or flat membrane—per manufacturer best practices.

4) Final Detail

Flashings, sealants, clean-up, and photo documentation.

FAQ

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most single-family homes take 1–3 days depending on size, access, and roof type.

Can you re-use my tile?

Often yes. We lift tiles, replace underlayment, flashings, and relay the tiles if in good condition.

Do you finance?

We can discuss payment options and stage work where sensible.
